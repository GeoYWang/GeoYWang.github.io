⚠️ WECHAT SECURITY ADVISORY ⚠️

Contact Method Deprecation Notice

NOTICE: WeChat is no longer provided as a public contact method due to significant security and privacy vulnerabilities documented below.

📋 Executive Summary

After thorough security analysis and multiple documented incidents, WeChat has been identified as an unsuitable platform for communications. This advisory details the specific vulnerabilities and privacy violations that led to this decision.

🔍 Documented Security Incidents

CVE-EQUIVALENT: Personal Data Exposure via Telegram Social Engineering Databases
• WeChat user data frequently appears in "social engineering databases" (社工库) on Telegram
• Attackers can perform "doxxing" (开盒) using WeChat numbers to obtain:
  - Real names and ID numbers
  - Home addresses
  - Phone numbers
  - Associated accounts
DATA BREACH: Shanghai Suishenma (随申码) Health Code Leak
• Massive data breach exposing millions of Shanghai residents
• WeChat integration led to correlation of health data with personal identities
• Demonstrated systemic vulnerability in WeChat-integrated government systems
PRIVACY VIOLATION: Unauthorized Hardware Access in Android
• Documented cases of WeChat accessing camera without user consent
• Microphone activation in background processes
• Continuous location tracking even when "location services" disabled
• Network traffic analysis reveals data transmission to undisclosed servers
CENSORSHIP ENGINE: Content Manipulation & Surveillance
• Automatic content deletion in WeChat articles (公众号文章)
• Real-time monitoring of Moments (朋友圈) with retroactive content removal
• Account suspension without appeal process
• Keyword filtering affecting private conversations
• Image recognition for "sensitive" content detection

Recent security research has also revealed:

  • Mini-programs lacking proper sandboxing
  • Voice message analysis and transcription without consent
  • Cross-app data sharing with other Tencent products
